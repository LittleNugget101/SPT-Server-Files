To Make the Server your own you must go into 

1) http.json

spt/Aki_Data/Server/configs/http.json  

change the backend ip to your own PUBLIC ip address.

2) port forward

you will also have to port forward 6969 on your router

very easy - go to your router portal, then find firewall options/port forwarding. 

add a new entry - Choose Both for UDP/TCP, then put 6969 in all fields.
remember to save the new entry.

3)

Launcher IP

change launcher IP address by going to spt/user/launcher/config.json

change Url to your PUBLIC IP + 6969

4)

In-game IP

my have to open f12 settings and find Fika

then change the ip to your Public IP.
